Real-time visualization of public transportation in the City of Cologne, Germany.

Many thanks to api.schienenliebe.de!

What is this?

This application visualizes public transportation vehicles moving through the City of Cologne, Germany.

Why?

This application is a proof of concept for the Open Data Day Cologne 2013.
